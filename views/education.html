Before becoming a technology enthusiast

Like most of young adults, I entered university expecting to get the knowledge to get my a job for the rest of my life.

During my studying years, I worked in sales, marketing, accounting and event planning.

However in all those jobs I was missing a connection with the craftsmanship behind the product I was promoting. I was lacking of sense for tasks.

My life changing influencers

I am eager to follow my intuition, so back in 2018 I gathered the courage to invest all my savings in a Web Development bootcamp.

It turned out to be one of the best decisions of my life, as have found in coding a true passion. I can stay hours focused in front of the screen, often at night.

In my personal work, like for food, I care for quality. I also love the complexity of human relations, so working inside a medium to big size team can stand out the brightest version of myself.
